Project detail

Credit Default Risk Analysis Dashboard

Built a financial analytics dashboard to identify credit default risks and support credit evaluation decisions.

Overview

This project analyzed customer financial data to uncover what drives credit defaults and delivered dashboards for credit risk monitoring.

Work completed

  • Collected and cleaned financial datasets for predictive analysis.
  • Performed exploratory data analysis to identify default risk signals.
  • Developed Power BI dashboards showing trends across income, employment, credit history, and debt ratios.
  • Documented findings and provided recommendations to improve credit evaluation.

Outcome

The dashboard gave stakeholders a clear view of risk factors and helped shape data-backed credit decisions to reduce default exposure.
