Project detail

E-commerce Customer Churn Prediction

Built a churn prediction model to identify high-risk customers and support targeted retention strategies.

Overview

This project predicted customer churn for an e-commerce business by analyzing customer behavior, purchase patterns, and engagement metrics.

Work completed

  • Collected and cleaned customer transaction and engagement data.
  • Engineered features representing purchase frequency, recency, loyalty, and product interests.
  • Built and compared churn models using LightGBM, XGBoost, and logistic regression.
  • Translated model outputs into business recommendations for retention campaigns.

Outcome

The model identified the highest-risk customer segments and the key retention drivers, helping the business prioritize interventions and reduce churn.
