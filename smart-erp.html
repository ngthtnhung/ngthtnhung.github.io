Project detail

SaigonBrew ERP & Business Intelligence Platform

Implemented an end-to-end Odoo ERP rollout for a manufacturing enterprise with a custom Quality Control module and consolidated operational reporting through Power BI dashboards.

Overview

This project delivered a complete ERP solution covering Sales, Inventory, Manufacturing, Procurement, and Quality Control. It was built to centralize operational workflows, improve data visibility, and support production quality through a custom QC module.

Work completed

  • Gathered requirements from manufacturing stakeholders to design multi-module ERP workflows.
  • Configured Odoo Sales, Inventory, Manufacturing, Procurement, and Quality Control modules.
  • Built custom QC logic for production traceability, defect tracking, and nonconformance handling.
  • Consolidated ERP data into Power BI dashboards for sales, procurement, manufacturing, and quality visibility.

Outcome

The ERP implementation helped standardize material flow and production processes, reduced manual handoffs, and provided business leaders with actionable insights through dashboards. The QC module gave the team a clearer view of defects and production quality trends.
